Natural Rubber

Description

Natural Rubber is obtained from the harvest of the sap of rubber trees that grow in countries with tropical climates. The sap of the rubber tree goes through the filtering process to clean contamination and then is dried. After that enter the shaping process. Southeast Asia is the largest supply for the world's rubber needs, one of the largest is Indonesia.
We provide SIR10, SIR20, RSS1, and RSS3 for domestic and international market.

Rubber Sheets

Description

Sheet Rubber can be used in many industrial sectors. Sheet rubber is commonly used because of the nature of rubber as an insulator that is easy to fold, print, cut and shape as needed.
